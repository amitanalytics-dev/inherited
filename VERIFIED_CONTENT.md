## BRAND STORY (real, from "Our Story" + "About Us" store pages — paraphrase only from this)
- Founder: Suruchi. Inspiration: her Indian grandmother, Leela ("leela-skincare" is the store's namesake). Grandma Leela used warm ghee pressed into face and hands every evening — a ritual passed down generations.
- Modern skincare (parabens, synthetic fragrance, harsh actives) left Suruchi's skin reactive; she returned to ghee. Inherited Skincare was born from that return.
- Washed ghee: clarified butter washed (traditionally 100 times in Ayurvedic practice) into a light whipped texture. Penetrates ALL SEVEN layers of skin — claimed as the only moisturiser known to do so.
- Ghee contains butyric acid (anti-inflammatory), conjugated linoleic acid, oleic acid, vitamins A, D, E, K. Comedogenic rating 1/5 — suitable for most skin, including sensitive.
- Ayurvedic use of ghee: 3,000–5,000 years (store uses both; "thousands of years" is safe).
- The Inherited promise: as few ingredients as possible, all natural, not tested on animals, sustainably sourced packaging. "We never take more than we need, or put in anything we don't need."
